  • Muay Thai
    This ancient martial art is also known as “the science of eight limbs”. In the art of Muay Thai the hands, elbows knees and shins are used as weapons, thus enabling the practitioner to make use of any part of his body a weapon to defend himself or a loved one.
  • Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu
    Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, also known as BJJ. Its founder Helio Gracie won numerous tournaments against bigger and stronger opponents using this martial art.
  • Boxing
    Boxing, also known as “the sweet science”, is one of the oldest combat sports in history. Created by the Greeks, the theory of boxing is to hit and not get hit.
  • Mixed Martial Arts
    Mixed Martial Arts, or MMA, is one of the fastest growing martial arts worldwide. It was originally based on the idea of taking the best techniques from various martial arts and merging them into a single dynamic system.
  • Fight Class
    Fight class is designed for those advanced students who wants to bring their training to a higher level or those who want to start competing in the combat sports.
  • Extreme Conditioning
    Extreme Conditioning is designed to ignite your explosive power and develop the speed in your body by working on functional muscle groups and core strengthening exercises.

Safety Reminder

Everyone within the Beast Gym premises are reminded to be mindful of and abide by our House Rules, Members or otherwise.

As a safety concern, all Members are kindly reminded to put on their Personal Safety Equipment (PSE) during class or training. PSE needed are as follows:

MUAY THAI / BOXING
Mouthguard, Handwraps (all gender).
Groinguard (Male only).

MMA
Mouthguard (all gender).
Groinguard (Male only).

Also, to ensure smooth conduct of class, Members who have been graded are to wear their relevant equipment to denote their rank, as follows:

MUAY THAI
Colored Prajiet worn around the glove of, or on the dominant arm.

BJJ
Colored belt, as applicable.
All awarded stripe(s), as applicable.
